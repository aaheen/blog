Bill Nom

1st place submission at Minnehack 2023, an annual hackathon at the University of Minnesota. Bill Nom takes in a URL for a bill on the Minnesota Revisor’s Office website, scrapes the bill’s contents, digests it with a lightweight Natural Language Processing (NLP) model, and outputs a summary that’s easier for laypeople to grasp than the full text. The NLP model powering Bill Nom is small compared to many language models, at 230 MB, it can easily run under the memory and processing power limitations of a consumer-grade laptop....
